# Pet Rock

A simple digital pet rock that lives in your browser.

**Live Demo:** https://pet-rock-black.vercel.app/

## What's This?

I was using an app called Widget Table and got bored, so I decided to try making something similar. It's a pet rock you can feed and watch get happy (or sad if you ignore it).

## What You Can Do

- **Name Your Rock** – Give your pet rock a name when you adopt it
- **Feed It** – Click the feed button to make your rock happy
- **Watch the Mood** – Your rock's face changes based on its happiness level
- **Happiness Tracker** – See how happy your rock is on the stats display

## How to Use

1. Use the welcome screen to randomize a rock name or start with the default.
2. Click `Start Journey` to enter the app.
3. In the Rock tab, type a kind message and click `Feed Rock` to boost happiness.
4. Watch the rock's mood and happiness bar update as you interact.
5. Open the Journal tab to write and save daily entries.
6. Use the Focus tab to track work sessions and add tasks.

## Why I Made This

I was messing around with Widget Table and thought it would be fun to try building my own version of a simple pet thing. Pet rocks seemed like a good place to start since they're pretty low maintenance.

## Tech Stack

- **HTML** – Structure
- **CSS** – Styling and animations
